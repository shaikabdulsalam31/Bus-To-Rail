About Us

Bus-TO-Rail is a website created with the motive of providing easier access to bus routes, rail routes, and BMTC routes for public transport travelers. Our aim is to make the journey planning process convenient and hassle-free.

Our website offers comprehensive information about various bus routes, rail routes, and BMTC routes, empowering travelers to make informed decisions about their commute. We understand the importance of having up-to-date and accurate information, so we strive to keep our database regularly updated.

In addition to route information, we also provide important helpline numbers for emergencies and other essential contacts. We believe that access to reliable helpline numbers is crucial for ensuring the safety and well-being of our users.

RailtoBus was created by Shaik Abdul Salam and Trupti Gouda, two enthusiastic 3rd-year ECE students studying at UVCE Bangalore. With a passion for technology and a commitment to improving public transportation, we embarked on this project to contribute to the community and simplify the travel experience for everyone.
